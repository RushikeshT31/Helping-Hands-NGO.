About Helping Hands

Helping Hands NGO is committed to improving lives by supporting education, healthcare, environmental protection and social welfare. Our dedicated volunteers work tirelessly to bring positive changes in society.

  • Free Education
  • Medical Camps
  • Food Distribution
  • Tree Plantation
